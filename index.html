Pat's Pro Shop

Since 1975 Pat Johns has been perfecting the art of ball drilling. He's local bowling icon who has been inducted into the Seattle's Bowling Hall of Fame. He is also the proprietor of our Bowling Center. His vast knowledge of bowling will aid you all along your journey. from buying your 1st ball & shoes, custom fitting the ball to your hand, lessons to help you establish a competitive edge in league or tournament play, or even just simple to expert questions about bowling. from start to end - pat is the man you want to speak to when looking to improve your game. He will always take thoughtful care & attention to your personal needs. Many great bowlers like Junior Gold Competitors & even a TV bowling celebrity come to get just the right fit at Pat Johns' Pro Shop!
